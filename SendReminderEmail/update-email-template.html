What should you record?

In short, record everything. You can filter out big accomplishments later but recording everything may bring out big trends in your growing responsibilities you might not have noticed otherwise.

How should I record my accomplishments

We recommend you use the Challenge-Action-Result model. The key here is to keep track of your positive effect of the company. That is the key to getting a raise or promotion.


The Challenge-Action-Result model in action:

  1. Think of a challenge you needed to resolve
  2. List the obstacles you had to overcome
  3. List the steps you took to overcome these obstacles
  4. Finally, record the result of your actions
